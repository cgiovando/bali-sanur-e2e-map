Sanur E2E Mapping

Bali end-to-end mapping exercise

The story

Over one week in Sanur, Bali, a complete humanitarian mapping workflow was run end-to-end for BPBD tsunami-evacuation planning - from flying drones to collecting infrastructure on the ground. This map brings the results together.

  1. Fly - community drones capture 5 cm imagery.
  2. Publish - imagery goes to OpenAerialMap.
  3. Map - buildings & roads traced into OSM (AI + a mapathon).
  4. Collect - field teams log critical infrastructure with ChatMap.
  5. Plan - data feeds evacuation planning in Field-TM.

Source plan: HOT Portal
